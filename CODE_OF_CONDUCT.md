# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Creatory a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussion and reviews.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation details, not people.
- Share context and evidence when proposing changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and PRs that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations can result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to maintainers via the security/contact channel described in `SECURITY.md`.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, code reviews, and community channels).
